About Me

Hello and Welcome Everyone!

My name is Liaina J. Mageo, but feel free to call me Lia. I am currently a student in Software Development, working diligently to become a Quality Assurance Tester. My late-Grandfather had worked for Microsoft's IT department and had always encouraged me to pursue a career in tech. Here I am today, learning about HTML, CSS, and much more.

I am a woman of Polynesian descent, and I aspire to share my knowledge and experience of tech with others in my community, as well as outside. I not only look forward to receiving hands-on experience, insight, or knowledge, but to also grow alongside my peers and enjoy the journey with them. Outside of school and work, I enjoy investing in my well-being, spending quality time with my family, sharing music, traveling, getting to meet new people, and listening to their stories.

Feel free to contact me via LinkedIn or explore my projects on GitHub.
